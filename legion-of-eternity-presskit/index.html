Full game description

Legion of Eternity is a singleplayer-focused story-driven card game with a setting that combines science fiction and cosmic horror.

Story

It is the 26th century. A science breakthrough of potentially limitless importance gives humanity the complete control of the Universe. It lasts for several days. Then the Universe retaliates.

Earth becomes a singularity. Insanity spreads through minds like a virus. Everything changes.

Those who survive begin a desperate war against the reality itself.

Card customization

Change card frames, skills, stats.

Strategic battles

Send units, summon structures, and execute actions to win turn-based card battles.

Crafting and looting

Obtain cards and card customization items from crafting and battle rewards.

Over ten hours of content

Story campaign, hundreds of battles, over fifty cards to obtain.
