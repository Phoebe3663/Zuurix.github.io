Game description

The Cursed Legion is a free single-player collectible card game that is currently in early alpha.

Game story

The Eclipse is about to plunge Human Worlds into darkness.

The Swarm is gathering, the Corporation is expanding, the Rebellion is rising, the Alliance is burning and the Curse is spreading. You were the first one to be touched by the Curse. You were chosen to be the Archlegionaire, to rebuild the Cursed Legion and lead it into the Eclipse to stop the forces of darkness from devouring Sun.

Features

  1. 70+ cards to collect

  2. Non-linear single-player campaign

  3. Epic sci-fi setting that pits dark magic against tanks, supersoldiers, and mutants

  4. Card collecting that is not hindered by microtransactions
