Magic of Autumn

About the game

They couldn't kill you, so they took your soul. You wandered soulless for hundreds of years until you found where they hid it. It's time to get it back.

Magic of Autumn is a singleplayer CCG with an overworld - and everything in the overworld can be turned into a card!
