About

'Ello! I am Ivar, also known as an independent game developer Zuurix. I am 21 years old and I am from Lithuania, which explains all the grammar errors.

I am officially making games since 2013, but I pretended to be game developer ever since I started using a computer. How does one pretend to be a game developer? It's simple: Open MS Paint and draw inventory screens, enemies, quest logs... I used to do it for hours every day until I have decided that it's time to stop faking and start making real games. Years have passed and now I proudly call myself game developer.

I have made four big games so far. Usually, I work alone on my games because I'm not a team guy. My goal is to make innovative games and I hope that in future my games will be well known. My biggest strength is my unwavering motivation. I never give up.

I love role-playing, tower defense and collectible card games, fantasy books, spaceships, magic, cats, Two Steps From Hell music and talking with my players.

I hate old stuff, anime, unoriginality and everything that has something to do with beetroots.

Oh, and sometimes I use 'Ello instead of Hello. Strange habit, I know.
