About

Hello!

I am Ivar, also known as an independent game developer Zuurix. Making games is everything to me and my ultimate goal is to become one of the best indie game developers in the world.

Video games have always excited me, I remember pretending to be a game developer ever since I got access to a computer. I used MS Paint to make interfaces for the games I pretended to be making.

In 2012 my favorite game RuneScape have gone through heavy changes that made it unplayable for me. Because I was unable to find a replacement for that game, I decided to start making my own games.

I learned programming basics with help of online courses and in September 2013 started making games with GameMaker: Studio.

In May 2016 I have released my first game on Steam Early Access. In September 2018 it has left Early Access and currently, it's my biggest game.

I am planning to release more games in 2019 and 2020.

I can code and make decent graphics. I consider myself pretty good at designing functional, balanced games.

When I don't make games, I play games, watch TV stuff or YouTube, sometimes read books. I'm a huge fan of everything sci-fi/fantasy.
