About

Hello!

I am Aivaras Klimas a.k.a. Zuurix, an indie game developer.

I was introduced to video games when I was 10 years old and since then I am extremely passionate about them.

I played them, I made sketches of them, I learned about them, and ended up developing them =]

In September 2013 I have completed several online JavaScript lessons, downloaded GameMaker engine and month later I had my first real game done.

After that I've been making games nonstop.

My first serious commercial game – Sector Six – was released on May 2016 as a Steam Early Access title.

In September 2018 it has left Early Access and currently, Sector Six is my most successful game.

Right now I am working on Light of the Locked World - an RPG inspired by old school MMOs like Ultima Online and RuneScape.
