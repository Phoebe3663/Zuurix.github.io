Full game description

The World Gate has malfunctioned and you are taken to the Locked World. As you learn to live in a new world, you notice that something sinister is going on. You have to find out what's happening before it's too late to save yourself.

Features:

Light of the Locked World has all of the beloved RPG features - quests, exploration, crafting, and deep character customization - executed in new ways.

Combat:

Real-time, only one button to attack, effectiveness is based on equipment stats and player's positioning skill.

Tactical real-time combat

Light of the Locked World is inspired by oldschool MMORPG RuneScape - its MMO aspect is transformed into local co-op and PVP.

Local co-op and PVP

Take the game to the next level by inviting another player to play with or against you. You have the freedom to compete for rare items, cooperate to overcome difficult tasks, steal, trade, duel, and more.
