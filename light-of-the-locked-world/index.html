Light of the Locked World

About the game

Light of the Locked World is a role-playing game in which a portal malfunction takes you to the world of Kayos. Unable to return home, you have to start a new life and face challenges that will redefine you.

Planned features:

Persistence: Your actions will make permanent changes to the world. Looted treasure chests never refill with goods, dead characters stay dead, resources are non-renewable.

Colonial world: Become a citizen of a world with a short history, coastal mining cities, developing democracy, magic-powered industry, lawless wastelands, demon-infested ruins, and uncertain, but promising future.

Complex characters: The game is populated by characters that belong to factions, can fight and help each other, pick up and equip items.

Play together: The game has a drop-in drop-out local multiplayer mode for two players that supports player-versus-player combat and does not require controllers.
