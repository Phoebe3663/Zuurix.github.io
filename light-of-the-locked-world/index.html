Full game description

Light of the Locked World is a fantasy RPG with co-op and PvP, focused on combat and exploration.

RPG

Fight enemies, find, loot, and craft equipment to customize your character with it, learn skills and spells, complete quests, and make decisions. This is RPG.

Co-op

Split screen and invite a friend to play with you! Share loot, cooperate to defeat dangerous enemy encounters, explore world's deadliest locations - unite and tame the world.

PvP

But you're not limited to unity. Enable PvP and turn game into an epic competition for limited resources and treasure!
