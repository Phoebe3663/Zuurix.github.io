Legion of Eternity

About the game

Legion of Eternity is a card game with deep customization, procedurally generated loot, and a story of cosmic horror. After apocalyptic cataclysm violates causality, a horde of mutants and machines is sent to wake the only thing that could save what remains.

We did a horrible mistake. An indescribable mistake. The Universe used to exist on the edge of an infinitely grander existence.

We have plunged it right into that abyss.

Our time is limited. I need to find a solution while I still can.

I remember something about what I did. There is something that lurks beyond everything, beyond what we considered to be real.

I have created you to activate the remnants of greatness. To do one last thing before we become infinite.

They have to know.
