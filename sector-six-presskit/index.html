Full game description

Sector Six is a side-scrolling, sci-fi shooter with a silhouette aesthetic, procedural loot, extensive customization, and story missions.

Features:

Save the Universe: Enter the distant future and join a desperate fight to stop the Eight Machines from destroying everything.

Build your spaceship: Customize the performance, appearance, and size of your spaceship, make it unstoppable by installing relics and activating set effects.

Modular difficulty system: Make the game as difficult as you want it to be. Higher difficulty means better rewards!

Master the ship combat: Learn enemy attack patterns to counter them, find the ability combinations that fit your playstyle, and obliterate everything that stands in your way with lasers and missiles.
